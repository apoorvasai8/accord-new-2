HOW TO APPLY

Admission Process

Our admissions process is a two-day event held twice a week on Monday-Tuesday and Thursday-Friday. This structure allows ample time for students and families to explore the school and complete all necessary steps. The admissions team will confirm your slot based on availability, ensuring a smooth and personalized experience for every applicant. Once your preferred date is selected, the team will coordinate all further steps to help you through the process.

Please Note: To check actual availability and book your slot, please proceed to the calendar on the registration page. We accommodate only 5 groups per day, and appointments are confirmed based on the availability shown on the registration page.

The first step involves filling out the inquiry form available on the admissions page, and verifying the email id and phone number. This allows parents and students to express interest and receive additional details about the school.

Once the inquiry is made, the registration process begins. Parents are required to upload the student’s mark sheets for the past three years and complete the registration payment of INR 30,000. After that, an appointment for a campus visit can be booked online using the calendar provided. Note: We welcome no more than three family members (child, father, and mother) for the campus visit, but grandparents are welcome to join.

Before the visit, the admissions team will review the application, including the student’s previous academic records and pre medical forms. Two-Day Visit Itinerary: Day 1: 09:00 am: Arrival at the campus. 09:10 – 11:50 am: Aptitude test for the student. 12:00 – 01:00 pm: Guided tour of the campus and a meeting with the Resident Medical Officer (RMO). 01:00 – 01:30 pm: Lunch break. 01:45 – 02:00 pm: Meeting with the Director of Activities. Day 2: 10:00 – 11:00 am: Meeting with the Section Coordinator & Headmistress. 11:15 – 11:30 am: Meeting with the Administration team.
